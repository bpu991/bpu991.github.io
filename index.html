PyPractice

PyPractice is a group project that I worked on with three other developers. It is a web-application that lets users practice python problems by utilizing an interactive terminal in the browser. It also lets users track their progress by allowing them to collect achievement badges whenever they complete a certain amount of problems or a certain amount of attempts.
